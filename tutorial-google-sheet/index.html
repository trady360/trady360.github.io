How to Create a Google Sheet for Trady

Step 1: Create a new Google Sheet

Go to sheets.new to instantly create a new spreadsheet.

Step 2: Rename the sheet

Click the title at the top-left and rename it to something recognizable, like Trady Sheet.

Step 3: Add a sheet named Trade

Click the + button at the bottom to add a new sheet and rename it to Trade.

Step 4: Add columns to the Trade sheet

Use these headers in row 1:

ID | SYMBOL | OPEN_TIME | OPEN_PRICE | QTY | SIDE | OPEN_REASON | STOPLOSS | TAKEPROFIT | CLOSE_TIME | CLOSE_PRICE | CLOSE_REASON

Step 5: Add another sheet named Capital

Click the + again and rename this one to Capital.

Step 6: Add columns to the Capital sheet

Use these headers in row 1:

DATE | METHOD | AMOUNT
