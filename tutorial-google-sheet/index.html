How to Share a Google Sheet as a Template

  1. Open your Google Sheets document that you want to share as a template.
  2. Click the "Share" button at the top right of the screen.
  3. In the sharing settings, make sure the document is set to "Anyone with the link" can view.
  4. Now, copy the link of your Google Sheet (it should look like: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/your_file_id/edit).
  5. To make the document accessible as a template, simply modify the copied link by replacing /edit with /template/preview. For example, the link:
    https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/10inEqn6GYgh2r7_FS0wKkikKkZyrfD3QKyna0vbBBDI/edit becomes:
    https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/10inEqn6GYgh2r7_FS0wKkikKkZyrfD3QKyna0vbBBDI/template/preview
  6. Now, you can share this new link with others. When they click on it, they will be prompted to make a copy of the document, which they can then edit and customize.
Important: Sharing as a template allows others to create their own copy without modifying your original document. Make sure the permissions are set to "View" to avoid accidental edits.
